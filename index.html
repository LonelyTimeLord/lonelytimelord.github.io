About

Hello, I am a 22 year old from Fleet, Hampshire. I am currently in my final year of studying Computer Games Design at Staffordshire University. I previously studied at The Sixth Form College Farnborough, when I took A levels in Art, Graphic Design, Maths and Computing. I have a passion for both art and games so to be able to combine the two is my goal.

In my spare time I like to make art, both digitally and traditionally, especially illustration. I also run a second Youtube Channel where I share my love for reading. I am a committee member for the university's archery society, and have been shooting for just over a year. My favourite games of all time are Team Fortress 2 and The Binding of Isaac.

Tank Model

First year university project, to produce high poly and low poly versions of a tank, which is then textured for use in an engine. This tank is based on a Panzer IV.

  • Date: July 2012

3D Head Model

Second year university project to create a self portrait model, including diffuse, normal, specular and opacity maps. Created using 3ds max for base mesh + retopologised mesh and Zbrush for the high poly sculpt

  • Date: December 2012

3D Body Model

Second year university project to create a 3D body model, including diffuse, normal and specular maps. Created using 3ds max for base mesh + retopologised mesh and Zbrush for the high poly sculpt

  • Date: April 2013

Velociraptor Model

3D Velocirtaptor model created using 3ds max for the base mesh + retopoligised mesh and Zbrush for the high poly sculpt + base diffuse texture.

  • Date: April 2013

TF2 inspired Animation

Second year university project to create a 3d animation using one or more characters from Valve's Dota 2.
