About Me

This is what I do
Hello, I am a 22 year old from Fleet, Hampshire. I recently graduated at Staffordshire University with a First class honours degree in Computer Games Design. I previously studied at The Sixth Form College Farnborough, when I took A levels in Art, Graphic Design, Maths and Computing. I have a passion for both art and games so to be able to combine the two is my goal.

In my spare time I like to make art, both digitally and traditionally, especially illustration. My other hobbies include reading and archery. I was a committee member for the university's archery society, and have been shooting for just over two years. My favourite games of all time are Team Fortress 2 and The Binding of Isaac Rebirth.
